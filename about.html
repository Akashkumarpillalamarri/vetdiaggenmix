About Us

Welcome to Andhra Pradesh's First Patented Nested PCR

Vetdiaggenomix is a veterinary diagnostic center specializing in providing advanced diagnostic services for animals. The company is led by S. Dileep, who holds a DAM and BFA, and serves as the Multimedia Specialist and Director & Digital Media Head. The founder of Vetdiaggenomix is Dr. mohana Sheela, who is also the founder and director of Jaya Teja Centre for Research and Diagnosis.

  • Molecular Diagnostics: Utilizing cutting-edge molecular techniques for accurate disease diagnosis.
  • Pathology Services: Comprehensive pathological examinations and consultations
  • Immunodiagnostics: Advanced immunoassay tests for various animal diseases.
  • Genetic Testing: Genetic screening and testing for hereditary conditions and traits.
  • Microbiology: Bacterial, viral, and fungal culture and sensitivity testing.
